Research

As to understand the users, I conducted user interviews and generated some pain points. This survey helped in determining issues users had with existing booking applications. I found that most apps provide the same flow of hotel bookings and users were unable to search & book the hotels in a quick way. I read reports about stress factors and how people were dealing with them, and conducted a competitor analysis to have a better idea of what the market looked like. This showed gaps in the market, as they were not often tailored to users, and none addressed clarity on searching & booking ‚Äî which was what most users needed.
