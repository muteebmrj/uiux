Background

Hey there! Once again, I'm Muteeb Mehraj, a multidisciplinary designer. I specialise in Brand Strategy, Identity Design, & UI/UX Design.

Two things you should know about me.

Firstly, I believe designs are simply tools for solving problems and driving results for people & companies.

Secondly, I'm not focused on the number of design skills I have. Instead, I'm focused on the design skills that best solve the challenges put before me; hence, my variety of skills.

The most important lesson I've learned throughout my career is I can't solve a problem I don't understand. Thus, I focus on first gaining a clear understanding of the problem any project hopes to solve. Then, with this understanding, I strategize on how to achieve the proposed solution or goal.
