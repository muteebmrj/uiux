Challenge

The challenge for this project was to create a design that matches the brand's values and communication with its audience. They had an incredible portfolio of work, but no online presence. They needed a website that matched the elegance and quality of their work to get new leads for future projects.


‍

Solution

Working hard with the Dexstudio team, I created several design directions and a site that would convey the company's image to its audience according to their expectations and ensure it was met as a priority. To do this, we relied on elegant typography and harmonious color choices.

Results

Finally, I created the website using Webflow. I made sure that the site was fully responsive and optimized for mobile devices, performing several breakpoint tests when finalizing the project.
