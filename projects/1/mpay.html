J&K Bank Mpay

Jammu & Kashmir Bank Limited (J&K Bank) is one of the oldest private sector bank in India, having its Corporate Headquarters in Srinagar, J&K with its Zonal Offices in J&K, Delhi, Mumbai. The aim of this project was to solve the problems of mobile banking experience and help the business grow digitally.

YEAR
2022
CLIENT
J&K Bank
Project
Mobile App
ROLE
UI/UX Designer

The Problem

No biometric authentication (Face Id/ Fingerprint).
No OTP based transaction verification.
Switching apps logs out the user.
The UI looks outdated.
No option to download A/c statement.
Recent transaction history limited to 5 which forces the customer to collect a bank statement from the branch.


‍

The Challenge

As with any redesign and UI/UX project, JK Bank Mpay online experience had it's own niche-related difficulties that we had to overcome. The core issues were all related to the complexity and the need to simplify and organize the visual information in a way that would create an experience which would be easy to use and fulfill the client's goals.


‍

The Solution

After a close analysis of the research data, we decided to create a plan of action that would bring us to a final redesign in aproximately 1.5-2 months. Following are the key actions which took place. UI was redesigned in a more modern way. Some critical functionalities were added to the app (like downloading a statement, biometric authentication, OTP based transactions) etc. UX was revamped from the groundup to simplify & improve the overall experience.

The Result

Finally, I redesigned the app and made sure it reflects the company's vision & also improves & simplifies the overall experience for the users.
